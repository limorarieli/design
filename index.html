About me

I am a designer with more than ten years experience specialising in active wear. My goal is to make innovative functional clothes that match the current market trends and perform research to predict and analyse trends based on customer needs.

Currently I am the product owner for multisport/running collections. Besides my work as a designer, I give my contribution to bring further our company's brand by improving the processes and strategies of collection development.
